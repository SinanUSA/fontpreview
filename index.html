Font Preview Tool

Enter your personalized text, select a font and size, then click "Add Line". You can add up to 5 lines. Adjust the font size with the + and - buttons, and remove lines as needed. Your text will appear on the wallet preview to the right.
Move button: Click "Move" for a line, then use the arrow keys to position your text on the wallet. Click "Stop Move" to finish.
